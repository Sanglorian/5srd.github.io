---
title: Jump
classes:
  - Druid
  - Ranger
  - Sorcerer
  - Wizard
level: 1
school: Transmutation
casting_time: 1 action
range: Touch
components: V, S, M
material_components:
  - a grasshopper's hind leg
duration: 1 minute
saving_throw: None
---

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.
